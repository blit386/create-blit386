# American English spelling

Canonical reference: [CLAUDE.md](../../CLAUDE.md) (Critical rules).

This repo's own source, docs, and scaffolder/kit prose use American English: `color`, `optimization`, `canceled`, never
the British equivalents. Exempt: literal third-party or spec-mandated names correctly spelled with a British `s`/`c` in
their own spec (for example Web Audio's `AnalyserNode`/`createAnalyser`) – do not "fix" those.

Cursor: `.cursor/rules/american-english-spelling.mdc` (always applied in this repo).
